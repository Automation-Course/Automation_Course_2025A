Welcome to PID!
Click Upload to run the program.
Enter your target angel .
Move the pendulum manually to +- 45 deg and let go. 
You will see the pendulum stabilizes around your target angle. 
The program will print the actual angle it stabilized on .
You can move the pendulum again to +- 45 deg and the program will print each time the angel after it stabilize. 
To start a new iteration and enter a new target angel click again the upload button .
